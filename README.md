#  *Die With Me*  
### A Short Horror Experience by **Last Life Studios**

---

##  About the Project  
**Die With Me** is a **short horror prototype** made in **Unity 2022**.  
It is not a full game — instead, it is a **small narrative experience** with:
- A short **cutscene-style story**
- A **small 3D explorable level**
- A **very light visual novel (VN) section**

The main character **Eris** dies at the beginning of the story…  
But later, she discovers something horrifying:

> **The one who killed her… was herself.**  

---

✔ Short gameplay — quick experience
